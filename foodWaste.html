Preventing Food Waste For Beginners

Context

I created an interactive list-based interace with React, composed of tying components to an internal data state. My interface allowed users to select food items from a list of what is in their refridgerator and aggreate the prep time for what is selected.

Applicability to College Students

Food waste is a huge environmental and ethical issue that is especially difficult to address when you are a new cook. College can be the first time students buy groceries and cook for themselves. Unfortunately, the result can be forgotten food that has to be thrown out and unhealthy dinners. Thus, I created an interface to filter and sort based on calories, expiration status, food group category, and prep time. The interface allows users to create a healthy dinner recipe with low food waste and plan for the prep time (helpful for beginner college cooks).

Process

(click arrows on the slideshow for the progression)

(1) I created a dinner item component to store data on the food.

(2) I created filtering and sorting to allow users to quickly identify what ingredients to use.

(3) I created a dinner recipe section that displays which items have been selected and aggregates their prep time.

Takeaway

It's important for the layout to be split into three parts that mirrors the three main user tasks: learn information about a food item, search for food items that satify low food waste and healthy criteria, and create a dinner recipe that helps beginner cooks by sharing the prep time needed.
