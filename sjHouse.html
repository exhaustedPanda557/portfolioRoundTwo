Finding Resources for Sexual Assault

Context

I selected a page of a website that had usability and accessibility issues and built a redesigned page to address the issues.

Applicability to College Students

Sexual assault is a huge issue on college campuses and society at large. It is important to connect students to resources and raise awareness of the issue by highlighting organizations that provide assisstance to those in college and outside of college. Sojourner House is a Providence support center for those struggling with domestic violence, sexual assualt, and human trafficking. Thus, I selected them for the redesign because I'm based at Brown University.

Takeaway

I improved accessbility with more high contrast colors and adding white backgrounds. To help students more quickly understand the importance and mission of the sexual assualt organization, I added a mission statement section that is viewed on the first screen. I moved to a more accessible section the user actions to "Get Help," "Support Us," and learn "About Us."
